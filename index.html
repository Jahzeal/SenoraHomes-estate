Welcome to
SenoraHomes

SenoraHomes is an award winning residential real estate developer, committed to helping people love where they live. We truly want residents and homeowners to cherish their living spaces. With a footprint in Ghana and Kenya we specialize in developing vibrant, moderately priced homes within secure gated communities. Our homes are renowned for their exceptional quality and design, seamlessly blending contemporary American architecture with timeless elements inspired by each unique location that we are present in.

In our carefully planned, quality communities, homeowners enjoy residences built with meticulous attention to detail, featuring integrated green parks and gardens, children's playgrounds, sports courts, swimming pools, and more. Our homes offer open floor plans and gourmet kitchens equipped with stone countertops and high-end modern appliances. Master bedroom suites are thoughtfully designed with dual vanities and all bedrooms provided with fitted wardrobes. From serene front porches to covered outdoor kitchens, from paved double car parking spaces to tree-lined sidewalks, we are raising the bar for both quality and community living.

About BlackIvy

SenoraHomes is a BlackIvy endeavor. BlackIvy is a values-driven U.S. company that builds, operates and scales businesses that drive long-term growth and resilient value in essential sectors – housing, food, healthcare, infrastructure and logistics – in Sub-Saharan Africa. Anchored in Ghana, Kenya and Tanzania, BlackIvy is relentless about quality, conscious about costs and driven to see its products and services enjoyed by more and more people. BlackIvy has offices in Accra, Dar es Salaam, Nairobi, New York and Washington, D.C.

GreenwichPark

At GreenwichPark, we are redefining modern living with a perfect blend of elegance, convenience, and tranquility. Nestled on 19.5 acres with 2.5 acres of stunning gardens and parks, GreenwichPark presents elegantly crafted 2-, 3-, and 4-bedroom townhouses tailored for the modern homeowner seeking comfort and style. GreenwichPark features a multifunctional clubhouse, multi-sports court, a children's play area, and a swimming pool—all crafted for a lifestyle you discern.

OyarifaPark

Welcome to OyarifaPark, an exceptional real estate development in Ghana. Explore our vibrant community of 200 residences, nestled within 15 acres of breathtaking landscapes with the backdrop of the Aburi mountains and just 30 minutes from Kotoka International Airport. Our homes embody the perfect fusion between American and Ghanaian architecture presenting an ideal home for sale, built to perfection. Immerse yourself in the comfort and convenience of a residential oasis.

AyiMensahPark

AyiMensahPark is a vibrant community of 200 residences, situated on 12.4 acres in the foothills of the Aburi Mountains, just 30 minutes from Accra Mall and Kotoka International Airport. As IndigoHomes inaugural project, it showcases our approach to curating homes and a community where residents love where they live. From open floor plans, manicured green spaces, a community sports court and swimming pool, this lovingly designed mixed-use community features both condos and townhomes.
