Welcome to
SenoraHomes

SenoraHomes is a leading Nigerian real estate developer specializing in residential properties that inspire a deep sense of home. Our core philosophy is to create living spaces people love. We develop sought-after, gated communities featuring vibrant, attainable homes renowned for superior quality and design. Our signature architectural style fuses contemporary American design principles with unique, locally-inspired elements, creating the ideal environments for building futures.

We have carefully planned and developed quality homes so homeowners can enjoy residence built with meticulous attention to detail, featuring integrated green parks and gardens, children's playgrounds, sports courts, swimming pools, and more. Our homes offer open floor plans and gourmet kitchens equipped with stone countertops and high-end modern appliances. Master bedroom suites are thoughtfully designed with dual vanities and all bedrooms provided with fitted wardrobes. From serene front porches to covered outdoor kitchens, from paved double car parking spaces to tree-lined sidewalks, we are raising the bar for both quality and community living.

About Andersoft

Senora Homes is an endeavor of Andersoft. Andersoft is a values-driven Nigerian -based company that builds, operates, and scales businesses that drive long-term growth and resilient value.
